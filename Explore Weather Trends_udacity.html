OBSERVATION¶

The global temperature doesn't varies much but Kanpur(city) shows the frequenct changes in the temperature over years.In my opinion 1 year smoothing level is clear as it has visible spikes. kanpur(city) temperature frequently changes over years and it continues increasing in the temperature.Global temperature doesn't changes frequently but its temperature is gradually increasing over the years.The observations using 1 smoothing steps are differentiable but 5,10 smoothing steps doesnt show enough differences.The above derived data shows Kanpur temperature has increased more than the temperature increased globally.The above data shows that there is much gap between the global and city(Agra) temperature.
